I Love Art

    I love art. Playing classical guitar and history of painting take most of my spare time.
    Back in Slovakia I played and sang in a band called Laktibrada (Laktibada is a nasty little creature that you can find in Slovak fairytales). We sang 6-voice a-cappella compositions and played a sort of alternative rock. Sounds suspicious? Never mind, you're not the only one, but I like that combination a lot.
    While in UK I play bass guitar in Red Wagon , a blues/jazz/folk trio. A very different stuff, but I enjoy it very much.
